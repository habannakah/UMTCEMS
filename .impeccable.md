## Design Context

### Users
University students (Club Representatives), Advisors, Student Council (MPP), and University Administration Staff (HEPA). They use this system for administrative tasks: submitting, reviewing, approving, and tracking event proposals. 

### Brand Personality
Professional, Structured, Reliable, Institutional.

### Aesthetic Direction
Enterprise/Production-grade UI that **strictly preserves the original wireframe layout**. The goal is to transition from raw brutalism (heavy black borders, overused bold text) to a refined, accessible, and clean enterprise aesthetic while honoring the lecturer's requirement to stay true to the original wireframe structure.

### Design Principles
1. **Strict Layout Preservation:** Do not alter the grid, flexbox structures, or general placement of elements.
2. **Enterprise Refinement:** Replace harsh `border-2 border-black` with softer, professional structural boundaries (e.g., `border border-slate-200 shadow-sm rounded-lg`).
3. **Typography Hierarchy:** Reduce the overuse of `font-bold` everywhere. Use `font-semibold` for headings and regular weights for body text to create a clean, readable hierarchy.
4. **Subtle Interactive States:** Add gentle hover states (`hover:bg-slate-50`, `transition-colors`) to buttons and clickable cards instead of raw structural shifts.
